Seahorses Forever

Seahorses Forever is a hangout comedy about a Los Angeles-based rock band moonlighting as babysitters. The show is a throwback to cartoons of the 1970s like Scooby Doo or Josie and the Pussycats except Seahorses Forever is a raunchy stoner comedy with risque jokes. It's Metalocaplypse meets Speed Buggy coming down from an LSD trip with the Muppet Babies!
